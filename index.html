    About Us

    College of Applied Science, IHRD will be hosting DRONA 2K18, a mega event which will bring together the disciplines of Electronics, Computer Science, Arts and Commerce. The one day event will happen on 9th of March 2018.

    The departments of CAS will be presenting many innovative and ground-breaking events under different brand names like HEXA (Electronics), ASTHRA (Commerce), TANDRA (Computer Science), and OERA (English)
